Golden Blend

 Description: Pale lemon, clear appearance. Light, soft citrus fruits lie in the unobtrusive but still present bouquet. Ideal for all those, who want to find a classical European White Wine with taste structure. Light pear hints in the aftertaste.

 Grape/Blend: Rkatsiteli, Mtsvane, Kisi, Khihkvi (25% each)

 Food recommendations: Food that can be paired with western Georgian food, e.g. light cheese, light Khachapuri, poultry, etc.

 Terroir: Teliani micro zone. Soil is brownish grey, alluvial, carbonated, large thickness, stony loam. Vineyard is very gentle slope (3%), exposure to south-east.

 Viticulture: Vineyard Expansion: Spanning across 3 hectares, accommodating four distinct grape varieties. Cultivated through the trellis system employing vertical shoot positioning, predominantly utilizing bilateral cordons or arched canes. The vineyard boasts six years of establishment, with a density of 3800 vines per hectare.

 Yields: approximately 4-6 tones per hectare.
