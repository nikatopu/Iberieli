Mtsvane

 Description: Very well balanced masterpiece of acidity, tannins, and the wished playful Mtsvane's grapes taste. not sweet fruit aromas like pear, apple and yellow plums (Mirabelles) are dancing with each other on the tongue.

 Grape/Blend: Mtsvane 100%

 Food recommendations: Food that can be paired with medium bodied white/amber wines, e.g. cheese, Khachapuri, poultry, maybe with heavier salads with heavier oils (extra virgin), maybe some steaks, etc.

 Terroir: Teliani micro zone. Soil is brownish grey, alluvial, carbonated, large thickness, stony loam. Vineyard is very gentle slope (3%), exposure to south-east.

 Viticulture: The vineyard extension spans 1 hectare and employs a trellis system with vertical shoot positioning, predominantly utilizing bilateral cordons or arched canes for cultivation. Established six years ago, the vineyard boasts a density of 3800 vines per hectare.

 Yields: approximately 4-6 tones per hectare.
