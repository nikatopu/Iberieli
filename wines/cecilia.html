Cecilia

LOCATION: Village Tsinandali, Kakheti Region, country Georgia

SUSTAINABLE/ORGANIC/BIODYNAMIC: Organic

SUSTAINABLE FOR VEGANS: Yes

ALLERGENS (EGGS AND/OR MILK): No

TASTING NOTE: Water white to pale lemon, clear, clear appearance. Aroma is moderate to high intensity; pine tree, apple, citruses and slight smokiness noticed. Taste of softness, grapefruit, citruses. Moderate to long finish.

Grape/Blend: 50% Rkatsiteli, 50% Mtsvane.

(LOCAL ) FOOD RECOMMENDATION: Light cheese, Khachapuri, fried chicken, beef.

-----------------------------------------------------

(MICRO) CLIMATE: characterized by moderate humidity, hot summer and moderately cold winters. The average annual sum of precipitation is 845 mm, 644 mm in the vegetation period. Draught is an issue sometimes.

TERROIR: Teliani appellation. Soil is stony alluvium, brownish grey, with sandy loam and calcium content (PH 8.5). Gentle slope (3%), exposure to south-east.

Viticulture: Trellis, vertical shoot positioning, grown mainly on bilateral cordons or arched canes. One year old seedlings planted end of 2014. Density: 3800 saplings/hectare.

Yields: approximately 25 hectolitres (wine) per hectare.

VINIFICATION: Natural Winemaking, namely:

  • Manual harvest, straight crash in the winery. Harvest time early september.
  • “Spontaneous” fermentation (indigenous yeast, naturally occurring), no selected/commercial yeast added.
  • Fermentation/maceration 7 month on small amount of grape must (skins, seeds, and stems)
  • Fermentation/maceration vessel - Kvevri (underground potery vessel) , 1200 liter capacity
  • Winery room air temperature controlled sometimes (during extreme weathers)
  • Maturation: grape must removed after 7 months maceration and maturation continued until August bottling
  • Maturation Vessel: Kvevri
  • Filtration - No
  • Fining - No
  • Sulphur - added 30mg/l during bottling
