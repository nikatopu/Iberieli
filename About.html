Zurab Topuridze

CEO of Iberieli

The man that started it and manages it all. Zurab Topuridze is the proud owner of Iberieli and a wine enthusiast, who seeks to introduce fine winery and traditions of making top quality drinks to the entire world.

Family of Iberieli

The family of Iberieli is spread amongst 3 different parts of Georgia, mainly in Guria and Kakheti. Our family doesn't consist of only the blood-related family members, but the entire groups of people helping the wine brewing and selling process.

Business of Iberieli

The business started off slow, selling only a couple of boxes of wine, but we managed to grow worldwide, currently selling in western countries such as - US, France, England and Italy.

Vineyard of Iberieli

The vineyard of Iberieli consists of 2 main parts - one in Guria, and one in Kakheti. The soil, the agriculture and the nature is what makes our vineyard and the wines stand out from others. Using nature's gift is a greatly siezed opportunity.
